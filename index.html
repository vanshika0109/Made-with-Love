About the author



I am a working professional who just loves to read and explore.
While exploring different genres and others, I slowly began writing and
sooner than later it became a hobby, and over time I fell in love with it.
I have written many pieces overtime but here in this book I have tried to compile
some poems that help to portray a story of a relationship,
i.e., from friendship to relationship to being strangers again.
